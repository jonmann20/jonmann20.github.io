The Legend of Zelda: Reservanted

A class project with the help of Austin Yarger, remaking part of the original Legend of Zelda plus an original level. Coming in at around 5,500 lines of code. How did they ever fit this all on an NES cartridge? I'm guessing they had more than 3 weeks.

  • Tech used in making the game:
  • Unity — A game development engine.
  • C#
  • Sprites from The Spriters Resource
  • Overworld tilemap idea from Invent with Python Blog
  • Audio from ZeldaDungeon.net and The Sounds Resource

Original game is copyrighted by Nintendo.
